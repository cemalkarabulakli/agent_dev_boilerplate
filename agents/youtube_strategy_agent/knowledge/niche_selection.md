# Niche Selection & Validation

Source: Turanlı Method, vidIQ niche research framework, Serkan & Burak Turanlı — 550+ Niche Framework

## The Foundational Rule

> Never create a channel without validating the niche first. The wrong niche worked hard = the right niche worked correctly from day one.

Niche selection determines: monetization ceiling, competition exploitability, content longevity, and automation potential.

## The 5-Dimension Niche Scoring Model

Score each candidate niche from 1–5 on each dimension. Total maximum: 25 points.

| Dimension | Score 1 | Score 5 | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Search Demand** | <1K monthly searches | >100K monthly searches | No demand = no organic growth |
| **CPM Potential** | <$2 CPM | >$15 CPM | Determines revenue per 1,000 views |
| **Competition Exploitability** | All channels have >1M subs, excellent content | Many channels have weak content despite decent subs | Reveals if you can realistically compete |
| **Content Longevity** | Trend-dependent (fades in months) | Evergreen (still relevant in 3+ years) | Affects long-term growth and passive income |
| **Production Feasibility** | Requires studio, expensive equipment, talent | Fully automatable with AI tools | Determines scaling potential |

**Minimum viable niche score:** 15/25
**Target niche score:** 20+/25

## High-CPM Niche Categories (Tier 1 CPM: $10–$30+)

These niches generate dollar-denominated, high-CPM traffic when targeting Tier 1 countries:

- **Personal Finance**: investing, budgeting, passive income, credit cards
- **Software / SaaS**: tutorials, reviews, productivity tools
- **Real Estate**: buying, investing, flipping
- **Business & Entrepreneurship**: side hustles, online business, freelancing
- **Digital Marketing**: SEO, email marketing, social media, ads
- **AI & Technology**: tools, automation, workflows
- **Career & Productivity**: remote work, job interviews, skills
- **Health & Wellness (non-medical)**: fitness, nutrition, mental health
- **Legal & Tax**: general guidance (not advice) for creators/businesses

## Medium-CPM Niche Categories ($4–$10 CPM)

- Education & Tutorials (non-tech)
- Home Improvement & DIY
- Cooking & Food
- Travel (English-language, Tier 1 audience)
- Relationship & Self-improvement

## Low-CPM Warning Zones ($0.50–$2 CPM)

- Entertainment / memes / reaction content (Turkish/local language audience)
- Gaming (unless Tier 1 audience, English-language reviews)
- Children's content (COPPA restrictions + low CPM)

## Niche Exploitability Analysis

A niche is exploitable when competitor channels show:

1. **High views-to-subscriber ratio gap**: A channel with 100K subscribers but videos averaging 50K views suggests audience loyalty but weak new viewer acquisition — a SEO/thumbnail problem you can exploit.
2. **Weak thumbnails**: Professional-looking thumbnails outperform hand-drawn or phone-screenshot thumbnails in the same niche.
3. **Poor SEO**: Videos not optimized for search = keyword gaps you can fill immediately.
4. **Content gaps**: Subtopics or questions the audience has that no top channel answers thoroughly.
5. **Outdated content**: Old tutorials still ranking for high-volume keywords = opportunity to publish updated versions.

## Validation Process (Before Starting a Channel)

1. Search the primary niche keyword on YouTube — note the top 10 channels
2. Check 3 channel metrics on each: subscriber count, average views per video, upload frequency
3. Open vidIQ or TubeBuddy — check keyword search volume and competition score
4. Search the top 5 keywords in the niche — can you write better titles? Create better thumbnails?
5. Check Google Trends for the niche — is interest stable or growing?
6. Estimate CPM: search "[niche] YouTube CPM" or use niche benchmarks above
7. Final score: calculate 5-dimension score. If below 15, pivot.

## The Faceless Niche Filter

If the goal is a faceless AI-automated channel, add these additional filters:

- Can the content be produced with AI voiceover + stock footage / screen recording?
- Does the content format allow AI-generated scripts without requiring lived experience?
- Is the niche English-language viable (Tier 1 CPM access)?
- Is the topic evergreen enough to justify automation investment?

Best faceless niches: Finance explainers, AI tools tutorials, productivity/software reviews, business case studies, history/geography explainers, motivational/self-help.
