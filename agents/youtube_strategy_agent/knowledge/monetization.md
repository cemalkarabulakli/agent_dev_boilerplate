# YouTube Monetization

Source: YouTube Partner Program official requirements, CPM benchmark data, Turanlı Method

## YouTube Partner Program (YPP) — Entry Requirements

### Standard YPP (Long-form Content)
- 1,000 subscribers
- 4,000 valid watch hours in the past 12 months
- AdSense account connected
- No active community guideline strikes
- Located in a YPP-eligible country

### Shorts YPP (Short-form Content)
- 1,000 subscribers
- 10,000,000 Shorts views in the past 90 days

### YPP Progress Tracking Formula
```
Watch Hours Progress: (Total Watch Hours / 4,000) × 100 = % complete
Subscriber Progress: (Current Subs / 1,000) × 100 = % complete
```

**Turanlı Method benchmark:** With a healthy niche + 2 videos/week + proper SEO, most channels reach YPP in 3–6 months.

**RPM vs CPM:** RPM (Revenue Per Mille) is what you actually earn per 1,000 views. It is typically 40–50% of CPM because not every view is monetized with an ad.

## Monetization Stack (Priority Order)

Layer monetization to reduce dependence on AdSense alone:

### Layer 1: AdSense (Foundation)
- Passive once YPP is active
- Scales directly with views
- Provides baseline revenue for reinvestment

### Layer 2: Affiliate Marketing (Fastest to monetize)
- Recommend tools, products, services in the niche
- Top affiliate programs: Amazon Associates, ShareASale, impact.com, direct brand deals
- Best-converting niches: software tools, finance products, physical products
- Can monetize before YPP eligibility
- Typical commission: 10–40% per sale

### Layer 3: Sponsorships (Medium-term, $500–$10,000+ per video)
- Inbound: brands find you once channel grows (>5,000 subscribers in a valuable niche)
- Outbound: pitch directly to brands whose products align with your content
- Platforms: Creator.co, Grapevine, FameBit (merged into YouTube BrandConnect)
- Rate benchmark: $20–$50 per 1,000 views for 30-second mid-roll spot in high-CPM niches

### Layer 4: Digital Products (Highest margin)
- Templates, checklists, ebooks, courses, community access
- Sold via Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, or own Shopify/website
- YouTube channels with 10K+ subscribers can earn $5K–$50K/month from products alone
- Best product formats for YouTube creators: templates, frameworks, accountability programs

### Layer 5: Membership / Community
- YouTube Memberships (requires 500 subscribers)
- Patreon / Buy Me a Coffee for supplemental income
- Best for loyal, engaged audiences — not for pure-automation faceless channels

## Revenue Estimation Model

```
Monthly AdSense Revenue = (Monthly Views / 1,000) × RPM
Monthly Affiliate Revenue = Clicks × Conversion Rate × Average Commission
Monthly Sponsorship Revenue = Sponsored Videos/Month × Sponsorship Rate

Total Monthly Revenue = AdSense + Affiliate + Sponsorship + Products
```

Example (Finance channel, 100K monthly views):
- AdSense: (100,000 / 1,000) × $10 RPM = $1,000/month
- Affiliate (2 links, 3% CTR, 5% conversion, $30 commission): ~$180/month
- Sponsorship (1 sponsor × $3,000/video): $3,000/month
- **Total: ~$4,180/month**

## Global Monetization Strategy

The CPM gap between Turkish-language and English-language content in the same niche is 5–15x.

If producing content in Turkish:
- Still optimize for Tier 1 audience by covering topics relevant to international audiences
- Alternatively: run two channels — one Turkish (community), one English (monetization)

If producing English-language faceless content:
- Target USA, UK, Canada, Australia keywords specifically
- Use Google Trends to confirm Tier 1 country interest before creating the video
- Verify audience geography in YouTube Analytics after first 10 videos — if Tier 1 % is below 40%, adjust keyword and topic strategy
