# Launch Metrics and Benchmarks

SOURCE: launch_campaign_manager knowledge base

---

## Core Launch Metrics

### Revenue Per Lead (RPL)
**Formula:** Total Launch Revenue ÷ Number of Leads (opt-ins or registrations)
**Why it matters:** Single most important benchmark for launch health. Tracks revenue efficiency regardless of list size.

| Level | RPL Range |
|-------|-----------|
| Below average | < $1 RPL |
| Average | $1–$3 RPL |
| Strong | $3–$10 RPL |
| Exceptional | $10+ RPL |

---

### Revenue Per Subscriber (RPS)
**Formula:** Total Launch Revenue ÷ Total Email List Size (at launch start)
**Why it matters:** Tracks how well the existing list is being monetized.

**Benchmark:** $1–$5 per subscriber per launch is typical for warm lists and validated offers.

---

### Cart Conversion Rate (CVR)
**Formula:** Number of Buyers ÷ Unique Sales Page Visitors × 100
**Benchmark by offer price:**

| Price Point | Below Target | Target | Strong |
|-------------|-------------|--------|--------|
| $97–$497 | <2% | 2–5% | >5% |
| $500–$2,000 | <1.5% | 2–4% | >4% |
| $2,000–$5,000 | <1% | 1.5–3% | >3% |
| $5,000+ | <0.5% | 0.5–1.5% | >2% |

---

### Opt-In Rate
**Formula:** Opt-ins ÷ Landing Page Visitors × 100

| Traffic Source | Below Target | Target | Strong |
|----------------|-------------|--------|--------|
| Cold paid traffic | <20% | 25–40% | >40% |
| Warm retargeting | <40% | 50–65% | >65% |
| Organic / email | <50% | 60–75% | >75% |

---

### Webinar Show Rate
**Formula:** Live Attendees ÷ Total Registrants × 100

| Type | Below Target | Target | Strong |
|------|-------------|--------|--------|
| Live webinar | <20% | 25–40% | >40% |
| Replay (video) | <30% | 40–60% | >60% |

**Note:** Show rate is heavily affected by: confirmation email sequence quality, reminder timing (24hr + 1hr), day of week (Tuesday/Wednesday/Thursday best), and time of day (12pm or 7pm local time best).

---

### VSL Completion Rate
**Formula:** Viewers who watched to CTA ÷ Total VSL viewers × 100
**Target:** 35–55% (depends on VSL length and hook quality)

**Critical diagnostic:**
- Completion rate <25%: Fix the hook (first 60–90 seconds) and the transition from problem to solution
- Drop-off before the 25% mark: Opening is too slow or credibility is weak
- Drop-off before CTA: Price reveal or offer presentation needs work

---

### Email Open Rate (During Launch)
**Target:** 25–35% on warm list
**Below 20%:** Subject lines, sending domain reputation, or list health issue
**Above 40%:** Exceptional engagement — list is warm and offer is well-matched

---

### Email Click Rate (During Launch)
**Target:** 3–6%
**Below 2%:** CTA placement, copy clarity, or landing page mismatch
**Above 8%:** Strong offer-to-audience fit

---

### Checkout Drop-Off Rate
**Formula:** (Cart Page Visitors - Checkout Completions) ÷ Cart Page Visitors × 100
**Target:** <50% drop-off (i.e., >50% of people who start checkout complete it)

**Causes of high drop-off:**
- Payment form is broken or confusing
- No trust signals at checkout (SSL, testimonials, guarantee)
- Price shock (value stack not established before checkout)
- Required fields feel invasive or excessive
- Mobile checkout not optimized

---

### Average Order Value (AOV)
**Formula:** Total Revenue ÷ Number of Buyers
**Levers:** Order bumps, payment plan vs. pay-in-full split, upsell acceptance rate

**Order bump benchmark:** 15–30% acceptance rate on relevant, low-friction bumps ($27–$197)

---

### Launch ROI (Paid Traffic)
**Formula:** (Total Revenue - Ad Spend) ÷ Ad Spend × 100
**Benchmark:** 3:1 ROAS minimum (revenue = 3× ad spend) for launch to be self-funding
**Target:** 5:1 ROAS on a validated evergreen launch

## Revenue Forecasting Model

**Formula:**
```
Revenue = List Size × Email Click Rate × Cart CVR × Price
```

**Scenario Planning:**

| Scenario | List | Click Rate | CVR | Price | Revenue |
|----------|------|-----------|-----|-------|---------|
| Conservative | 2,000 | 3% | 1% | $2,000 | $1,200 |
| Base | 2,000 | 4% | 2% | $2,000 | $3,200 |
| Stretch | 2,000 | 6% | 3% | $2,000 | $7,200 |

**Close window multiplier:** Add 30–40% to base forecast for close window conversion surge.
