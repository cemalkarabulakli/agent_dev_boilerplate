# Launch Assets — L.O.A.D. Framework

SOURCE: launch_campaign_manager knowledge base

---

## L.O.A.D. Asset Categories

Every launch requires four asset categories. No launch should open cart until all critical (C) assets are complete. Warning (W) assets should be complete before paid traffic spend exceeds $500.

---

## L — Lead Generation Assets

### Opt-In / Registration Page
**Severity:** Critical
**Purpose:** Convert cold and warm traffic into subscribers or webinar registrants

**Required elements:**
- Headline: specific outcome promise (what they will learn or get)
- Sub-headline: who this is for and the timeframe
- Opt-in form: first name + email (phone optional for SMS)
- Social proof: number of students, logos, or testimonial
- Privacy notice: "No spam. Unsubscribe anytime."

**Page benchmarks:**
- Cold traffic opt-in rate: 25–40%
- Warm traffic opt-in rate: 50–70%
- A/B test the headline first — it drives 80% of opt-in rate variance

### Thank-You Page
**Severity:** Warning
**Purpose:** Confirm registration, deliver the next step, pre-frame the webinar/content

**Required elements:**
- Confirmation message
- Clear instruction: "Check your inbox / Add to calendar"
- Pre-frame video (optional but increases show rates by 10–20%)
- Social share button (optional)

### Confirmation Email Sequence (3 emails minimum)
**Severity:** Critical
**Emails:**
1. Confirmation + access link (send immediately)
2. Reminder (24 hours before event)
3. Day-of reminder (1–2 hours before event)

**SMS reminder (optional):** Increases show rate by 15–25% if opted in.

## O — Offer Presentation Assets

### Sales Page / VSL Page
**Severity:** Critical
**Purpose:** Present the full offer and convert interested visitors to buyers

**Sales page structure (long-form):**
1. Headline: transformation promise
2. Video (VSL) or written narrative: story + problem + mechanism + solution
3. What you get: core program breakdown with benefit-driven bullets
4. Bonus stack: each bonus with name, value, and benefit
5. Testimonials: 3–5 specific, result-focused
6. Price reveal with payment options
7. Guarantee section: specific, risk-reversing
8. FAQ: 5–8 questions that address real objections
9. Final CTA with deadline countdown

**VSL specifications:**
- Optimal length: 20–45 minutes for high-ticket ($2,000+)
- Hook (first 90 seconds): must create curiosity, not a generic intro
- Structure: Hook → Problem → Mechanism → Proof → Offer → Close → CTA
- CTA placement: After proof section AND after price reveal
- Mobile viewing: VSL must autoplay or have prominent play button on mobile

### Checkout / Order Page
**Severity:** Critical
**Purpose:** Complete the transaction

**Required elements:**
- Order summary (product name, price, what is included)
- Payment form (Stripe, PayPal, or similar)
- Guarantee badge
- Security trust badges (SSL, secure checkout)
- Testimonial (1–2 short ones near the form)
- Mobile-optimized layout

**Order bump (optional but recommended):**
- Add-on priced at $27–$197
- Complement to the main offer (not a replacement)
- Single checkbox on order form
- Benefit-focused copy in 3–5 lines

### Webinar Deck (if running live or automated webinar)
**Severity:** Critical (if webinar is the offer vehicle)
**Structure:**
1. Title slide with hook and presenter credibility
2. What you will learn (3–5 outcomes)
3. Credibility and story: why am I qualified to teach this?
4. Content block 1: The opportunity / worldview shift
5. Content block 2: The mechanism explained
6. Content block 3: The proof (case study walkthrough)
7. Transition to offer: "Now I want to show you how to implement this..."
8. Offer reveal: full stack, bonuses, price, guarantee
9. Q&A and close

**Webinar benchmark:** 10–20% conversion rate on live webinar with warm audience is strong.

## A — Activation (Email) Assets

See `email_sequences.md` for full sequence templates.

### Required sequences:
1. Pre-launch content (PLC) — 3–5 emails (Critical)
2. Open cart announcement — 1 email (Critical)
3. Open cart nurture — 2–4 emails (Critical)
4. Close sequence — 3 emails (Critical)
5. Buyer onboarding — 3–5 emails (Warning)
6. Non-buyer follow-up / waitlist — 1–2 emails (Warning)

### Email platform requirements:
- List segmentation: buyers vs. non-buyers
- Automation trigger: purchase → buyer sequence fires, stops non-buyer sequence
- Open rate tracking and click tracking enabled
- Custom send times per timezone (optional but recommended)

## D — Distribution (Traffic) Assets

### Organic Content Calendar
**Severity:** Warning
**Purpose:** Warm the audience across social, YouTube, or podcast during pre-launch

**Recommended cadence:**
- Social posts: 1/day during pre-launch window (reinforce PLC themes)
- YouTube/podcast: 1 new piece aligned to the mechanism (publish 7–10 days before cart open)
- Stories / reels: daily during pre-launch and cart open

**Content types:**
- Mechanism explanation (educational)
- Case study or transformation story
- "Coming soon" tease with curiosity hook
- FAQ or myth-busting
- Behind-the-scenes (builds trust)

### Paid Traffic Setup
**Severity:** Warning (Critical if organic list < 1,000)
**Platforms:** Meta Ads, Google Ads, YouTube Ads

**Pre-launch paid traffic:**
- Objective: opt-in or registration (lead generation)
- Audience: cold lookalike + interest targeting
- Creative: short-form video (30–60s) or image with mechanism hook
- Landing: opt-in page only — NOT directly to sales page
- Budget: $20–$100/day during pre-launch (scale based on opt-in rate)

**Retargeting during cart open:**
- Audiences: page visitors, video viewers (75%+), email list upload
- Creative: direct response — offer, deadline, testimonial
- Budget: 2–3× pre-launch daily budget during cart window
- Ad fatigue watch: refresh creatives every 72 hours during live launch

### JV / Affiliate Outreach
**Severity:** Warning (required for JV launch model)
**Materials needed:**
- Affiliate swipe emails (2–3 pre-written emails for partners)
- Promo calendar with key dates
- Partner portal with link, commission info, and leaderboard (optional)
- Pre-launch briefing call with top partners
