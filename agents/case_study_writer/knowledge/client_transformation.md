# Client Transformation Framework

## The Before State Must Include
- A specific number representing the pain (revenue, leads, hours wasted, conversion rate, etc.)
- What they had already tried that didn't work
- The cost of staying stuck (financial, emotional, time)
- A description specific enough that the target avatar recognizes themselves

## The After State Must Include
- Primary result: one specific number + timeframe
- 2–3 secondary results (side effects of the transformation)
- What daily life looks like now vs. before

## The Mechanism Must Include
- Exact steps in chronological order
- Which specific part of the program or framework they used
- What was counterintuitive or different from what they'd tried before

## The Obstacle Must Include
- The specific moment it almost didn't work
- What the client did (or the program helped them do) to get past it
- Why this obstacle makes the result more credible, not less

## The Catalyst Must Include
- What finally made them act (not generic motivation — a specific trigger)
- What belief or fear they had to push through to start
