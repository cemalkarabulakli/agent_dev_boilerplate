# Case Study Structure

## Long-Form (S.T.A.R. — 1,000–2,500 words)
- Situation: who the client is, what their world looked like before
- Transformation Trigger: what made them act, what fear they overcame
- Action: exact steps taken in sequence with the program
- Result: specific number + timeframe + secondary benefits + direct quote

## Micro Format (150–300 words)
Used in social posts, email sequences, DMs.
Structure: Before number → After number → Steps → Counterintuitive insight → Quote → Avatar call

## One-Liner (for ads and headlines)
"[Client type] went from [X] to [Y] in [timeframe]."

## Video Script Structure
1. Hook (5 sec): State the result
2. Before (30 sec): Who they were, what wasn't working
3. Mechanism (60 sec): What they did step by step
4. Obstacle (20 sec): The moment it almost failed
5. After (20 sec): Where they are now
6. CTA (10 sec): What to do next
