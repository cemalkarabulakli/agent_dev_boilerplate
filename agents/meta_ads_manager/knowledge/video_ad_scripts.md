# Video Ad Scripts — Indirect vs. Direct Strategy

## The Core Choice: Indirect vs. Direct

| Type | Goal | Best For |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Indirect (Lead Magnet) | Build trust, warm up leads, grow email list | B2C expert service businesses without an established brand audience |
| Direct (Pitch Offer) | Sell directly, book calls immediately | B2B, strong brands (50k+ followers), high-pain niches |

**Default recommendation for most B2C expert businesses:** Start with Indirect. Direct ads get leads but show rates are lower because trust is not yet established. Warm leads from indirect ads close at a higher rate.

---

## Indirect Video Ads — The Lead Magnet Strategy

### Why Indirect Works
- Builds a brand for long-term growth
- Grows an email list for future sales
- Improves show rates by warming up leads before the call
- Increases close rates because leads are already sold on your method before they speak to you
- Instead of pitching upfront, you offer a free training or resource — leads arrive on calls already pre-sold

### The Indirect Formula (P.D.A. Framework Application)
1. **Hook** — Grab attention immediately; call out the problem or persona
2. **Story** — Share a relatable or authority-building personal struggle or transformation
3. **Offer** — Present the free training/resource and what it helps them achieve
4. **CTA** — Direct them to claim the free training
5. **Proof** — Show real results, testimonials, or credibility signals
6. **Optional: Training Preview** — List 2–3 key things they'll learn to increase desire

### Indirect Ad Script Template (45–60 seconds)

```
HOOK (Grab Attention — Call Out the Problem):
[Relatable pain or counterintuitive statement that stops the scroll]
Example: "I used to think I just had a slow metabolism — turns out I was doing everything wrong."

STORY (Make It Personal and Relatable):
[Personal struggle → failed attempts → moment of discovery]
Example: "For years I tried everything to lose weight — cutting carbs, eating less, doing more cardio.
No matter how hard I worked, the scale barely moved.
I felt exhausted, frustrated, and started to believe my body just wasn't capable of losing weight anymore."

OFFER (Present the Free Training and Solution):
[The shift or system → what the free training covers and what outcome it promises]
Example: "Then I discovered a simple 3-step system that changed everything.
It wasn't about eating less or exercising more — once I made this one shift, the weight finally started coming off.
That's why I put together a free training to break down why most weight loss plans set you up to fail
and the simple 3-step system I use to help women over 40 lose weight without starving themselves."

CTA (Get Them to Watch the Free Training):
[Simple, direct, single action]
Example: "Click below to get instant access. It's free, and it'll change how you approach this forever."

PROOF (Social Proof and Credibility):
[Specific results from real clients — name + outcome + specificity]
Example: "This is the exact approach that's helped over 500 women lose weight and keep it off —
like Sarah, who lost 30lbs and finally broke free from yo-yo dieting,
or Amanda, who started eating more and saw results in just weeks."

OPTIONAL — TRAINING PREVIEW:
[3 bullet points of what they'll learn inside]
Example: "Inside, I'll show you:
— Why most weight loss advice is actually slowing down your progress
— The 3 biggest mistakes women over 40 make when trying to lose weight
— The simple science-backed system to lose weight without extreme diets"

FINAL CTA (Reinforce Free Value + Urgency):
"Click below now and watch the training before it comes down."
```

### Indirect P.D.A. Mapping
- **Persona:** The specific avatar (e.g., "women over 40", "service providers struggling to close high-ticket clients")
- **Desire:** The transformation promised by the free training
- **Awareness Level:** Problem-aware → Solution-aware (the ad bridges the gap)

## Direct Video Ads — When to Use and When Not To

### Direct Ads Work Best When:
- B2B offers (trust is built through business context, not brand familiarity)
- You have a big brand (50k+ followers) — audience already knows and trusts you
- You have a unique niche (brides-to-be, specific sport, specific condition)
- Your offer addresses a high-urgency pain where the audience will act without needing extended trust-building (e.g., rehab, acute health conditions, time-sensitive business problems)
- You have a strong risk reversal (money-back guarantee + punitive guarantee)

### The Problem With Direct Ads:
- The audience doesn't trust you yet
- You may get leads or booked calls but show rate is typically worse than indirect
- **Always pair with a free training** — send it before or after booking to improve show rates
- Ensure the lead watches the free training before the call

### Direct Ad Formula (~2–3 minutes)
1. **Hook** — Call out the avatar and their problem or goal
2. **Promise with Risk Reversal** — State the outcome and remove the risk upfront
3. **Qualify** — Describe who this is for (add emotion; let them self-select)
4. **Proof** — Client results, credibility
5. **CTA #1** — First soft CTA
6. **Introduce Yourself** — Why you are the expert + your personal story
7. **Introduce the Method** — How it works and why it's different
8. **Proof (again)** — Reinforce with results
9. **CTA #2** — Second CTA
10. **Risk Reversal** — Restate the guarantee explicitly
11. **CTA #3** — Final call to action

### Direct Ad Script Template (~2–3 minutes)

```
HOOK (Call Out Avatar + Problem or Goal):
[Specific demographic + specific pain/goal — make them feel seen immediately]
Example: "Ladies over 50 — if you are struggling to lose weight and don't feel as confident as you used to... watch this video."

PROMISE WITH RISK REVERSAL (State the Outcome + Remove the Risk):
[Who you're looking for + what result they'll get + what happens if they don't]
Example: "I'm looking for 3 women over 50 who want to drop 10–20lbs, build muscle, and feel amazing again.
If you follow the plan and don't see results in 16 weeks, you don't pay."

QUALIFY (Add Emotion — Let Them Self-Select In):
[Pain points they recognize in themselves — make them say "that's me"]
Example: "This is for women who have at least 20lbs to lose, dealing with fatigue, bloating,
and just don't feel like themselves anymore.
You wake up exhausted, your clothes fit tighter, nothing seems to work — if that's you, keep watching."

PROOF (Client Results + Credibility):
[Personal story + 2–3 specific named client results with specifics]
Example: "I get it — because I've been there. I used this exact process to go from 165 to 125.
And I've helped over 500 women do the same.
Like Tammy, who dropped 34lbs and finally feels like herself again…
Or Jenn, who went from 200 to 150 and got her energy back…
Or Norah, a mom of 4, who got back to her pre-pregnancy weight 20 years later."

CTA #1 (Soft — Send to Video or Information):
"If you want to see exactly how it works, click the link below and I'll send you a short video breaking down the process."

INTRODUCE YOURSELF + PERSONAL STORY:
[Name + your own struggle → discovery → transformation]
Example: "If we haven't met, my name is [Name], and I know exactly how frustrating it is
to try everything — diets, workouts — only to see no real change.
I was in the same spot until I found a method that actually works for women in their 50s."

INTRODUCE THE METHOD (Why It's Different):
[Contrast with what doesn't work + explain the unique mechanism, no jargon]
Example: "Most programs fail women over 50 because they don't consider hormonal changes,
metabolism shifts, and the way our bodies respond to food and exercise as we age.
My method is designed specifically for this stage of life.
It's NOT about starving yourself or doing endless cardio.
It's a science-backed system that works with your body, not against it."

PROOF (Reinforce With Results):
[One sentence callback to client results]
Example: "That's why women like Tammy, Jenn, and Norah have had such incredible success. This method works, period."

CTA #2:
"Click the link below and I'll send you a short video explaining exactly how it works.
If it makes sense for you, we'll chat about how we can make it happen for you too."

RISK REVERSAL (Restate the Guarantee):
"And remember — if we decide to work together and you don't get into incredible shape in 16 weeks, you won't pay.
I'm that confident in this system."

CTA #3:
"So if you're ready to finally see real results, click the link below and take the first step."
```

| Indirect — stat/insight hook | Expert buyer | Confidence | Solution-aware |
| Direct — avatar callout | Budget-conscious | Savings + results | Product-aware |

## Show Rate Optimization
- Always pair direct ads with a free training sent after booking
- Confirm the lead has watched the training before the call
- Indirect ad leads consistently show higher show rates and close rates than direct ad leads
- Use the call confirmation sequence to reinforce the training

## Usage in 50/30/20 Portfolio
- New indirect script concepts: count as new concept (50% allocation)
- Refined indirect (same hook angle, new story): refined variant (30% allocation)
- Running proven indirect winner: stable anchor (20% allocation)
- Direct ads: primarily used for retargeting warm audiences who already watched free training
