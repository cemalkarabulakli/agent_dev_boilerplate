# Meta Ad Policies — Compliance Reference 2025

## Absolutely Prohibited Content

Immediate ad rejection and potential account suspension:
- Counterfeit goods
- Tobacco, e-cigarettes, vaping products (without written Meta permission)
- Illegal drugs and drug paraphernalia
- Weapons, ammunition, explosives (without authorization)
- Unsafe or deceptive supplements
- Explicit adult content
- Spy/surveillance software that hides from the device user
- Payday loans, paycheck advances, bail bonds
- Multi-level marketing with unrealistic income claims
- Content discriminating based on protected characteristics
- Hate speech and violence incitement

## Personal Attributes Policy — Frequently Violated

Meta prohibits ads that **assert or imply personal attributes** of the audience.

Prohibited attributes:
- Race or ethnicity
- Religion or beliefs
- Age
- Sexual orientation or practices
- Gender identity
- Physical or mental health / medical conditions
- Disability
- Vulnerable financial status
- Voting status / political affiliation
- Trade union membership
- Criminal record

### Violation Examples and Compliant Alternatives

| Violating Copy | Compliant Alternative |
| --- | --- |
| "Are you struggling with diabetes?" | "Support healthy blood sugar levels" |
| "Single and looking?" | "Find meaningful connections" |
| "As a Christian, you'll love..." | "Timeless values, modern life" |
| "Feeling financially stressed?" | "Take control of your financial future" |
| "Dealing with anxiety?" | "Tools for everyday calm and focus" |

**Test:** Does the ad assume something about the viewer's personal situation? If yes, rephrase as a general benefit.

## Special Ad Categories — Require Additional Compliance

### Financial Products and Services (expanded October 2024)
- Covers: banking, insurance, loans, investments, credit, crypto
- Restrictions: No demographic targeting by age/gender/zip code
- Crypto: Requires written approval from Meta before running
- Prohibited: Guaranteed returns, debt relief misrepresentation
- Required: Clear disclosure of fees, terms, and risks

### Health and Wellness (tightened January 2025)
- No side-by-side before/after transformation images for weight loss or anti-aging
- No close-up images of pinched fat or specific body areas
- No body shaming language
- No medical terminology implying treatment ("treats inflammation" → "supports joint health")
- January 2025: Pixels and CAPI blocked for domains associated with implied health data

### Housing (Fair Housing Act compliance)
- No targeting by race, national origin, religion, sex, disability, family status
- No zip code targeting that creates disparate impact
- Must use Special Ad Audience (not standard LAL) for similar audiences

### Employment
- No age, gender, or zip code targeting restrictions
- Must use Special Ad Audience for lookalike audiences

### Political / Social Issues
- Requires authorization in country where ad runs
- Mandatory "Paid for by" disclosure label
- Categories: electoral issues, civil rights, immigration, healthcare, economy

### Alcohol
- Must target 21+ (US), 18+ (varies by country)
- Cannot imply health benefits
- Cannot show consumption before driving, during pregnancy, or targeting youth

## Content Quality Requirements

Ads rejected for:
- Excessive capitalization (ALL CAPS throughout body copy)
- Excessive symbols (!!! or ???)
- Before-and-after images without documentation for health/beauty products
- Generic stock photos misrepresenting product
- Vague clickbait CTAs ("Learn the secret," "Click to find out")
- Text overlay exceeding 20% of image area
- Unsubstantiated claims: "best," "guaranteed," "instant results," "#1" without proof
- Misleading prices or promotions vs. landing page
- Broken or inoperable landing page
- Landing page that redirects to different content than ad promises
- AI-generated content without "AI-generated" label (2025 requirement)

## Account-Level Compliance (2025 Checklist)

- [ ] Business Verification complete in Business Manager
- [ ] Ad Account Health Score monitored (low scores restrict delivery)
- [ ] Landing page loads within 3 seconds, matches ad promise
- [ ] Privacy Policy displayed on landing page (required for any data collection)
- [ ] Domain verified in Business Manager
- [ ] Events Manager: Check Sensitive Category status — verify not incorrectly flagged
- [ ] Appeal process: File within 24 hours of incorrect rejection (appeals take 3–7 business days)

## Policy Check Before Any Campaign Launch

Before launching or recommending a campaign, verify:
1. Is this a Special Ad Category? (Finance, Health, Housing, Employment, Political)
2. Does any ad copy assert personal attributes of the audience?
3. Does the landing page match the ad's promise?
4. Are all claims in the ad copy supportable with evidence?
5. Is AI-generated content labeled as such?
6. Does the account have Business Verification complete?
